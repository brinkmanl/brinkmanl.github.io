Who I Am

My name is Lora Brinkman. I live in Columbia, Missouri. I have been married to Chris for 18 years. I have a stepdaughter, Callie. We have a Shiba Inu, Niko, and a Siamese cat, Fast Eddie.

Check out my resume

Where I've Been

I have worked in Information Technology for over 20 years. I have developed both websites and web applications. I began my career as an application programmer. I then worked for a small non-profit organization as a web application developer. I started working at the University of Missouri in 2019 as a database programmer. In 2020, I decided to further my education. I received a Bachelor of General Studies from the University of Missouri (M-I-Z!) in May 2023.

What I Do

I am currently working on a Master of Learning Technology and Design degree. I have a YouTube channel, Lora B Flosstube. I share various craft ideas, give tips and tricks, and attempt to utilize learning techniques to help viewers improve their skills. I have been improving the quality of information shared on my channel with learning techniques discussed in the graduate classes I am taking. I enjoy quilting and cross-stitching. I'm involved with a local quilters' guild. I enjoy supporting the community through charitable efforts. I enjoy going to the gym for stress relief. My husband and I love live music, fine dining, and car shows. These activities bring happiness and balance to my life.
