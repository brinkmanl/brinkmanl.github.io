Booneslick Trail Quilters' Guild, 2018-present

  • 2024-2025: Past CEO, Membership Guide Chair
  • 2023-2024: CEO, Membership Guide Co-Chair, Quilt Show Committee - Publications
  • 2022: Quilt Show Committee - Publications
  • 2018-2020: Newsletter Editor

The Booneslick Trail Quilters' Guild is the oldest quilting guild in Mid-Missouri, beginning in 1978. I joined the guild to improve my quilting and network with other fiber artisans. I have formed valuable friendships and had numerous opportunities to support the organization. Since the guild is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization, part of the mission is to educate and support our community. We have donated hundreds of quilts and spread the knowledge of this pastime at various events around the central Missouri area. I have been personally involved in creating and collecting over 100 quilts donated annually to an Easter basket project for foster children since 2020.

Girl Scouts will forever have a special place in my heart and fulfilled my desire to help others and give back to our community.
